I am currently working as an AI Scientist at LG AI Research (EXAONE Lab), contributing to EXAONE 4.5 — a hybrid VLM that unifies reasoning and non-reasoning capabilities in a single vision-language model — and applying it to vertical domain projects across finance, automotive, life science, and defense.
